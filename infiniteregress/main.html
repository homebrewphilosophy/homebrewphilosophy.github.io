One day, you read some words written on a website.

The words were talking about you, and about themselves.

You thought it was a neat gimmick. Or maybe you thought it was trying too hard.

Either way, there was some purpose behind them.

The person who wrote them wants something.

What does he want?

Does he want to manipulate you?

Does he want to express something to you?

Does he want to sell something to you?

Does he want to tell you a story?

Does he want to make you think like him?

Does he know what he wants?

You’re not totally certain.

And if you are, maybe you shouldn’t be.
